Welcome to SDFC Bank

SDFC is a leading public sector bank in India with a rich history and a strong presence both nationally and internationally. Established in 1806 as the Bank of Calcutta, SDFC has played a crucial role in the economic development of the country.

Our mission is to be the bank of choice for our customers by delivering excellent products and services. With a wide range of banking and financial services, we cater to the diverse needs of individuals, businesses, and communities.

At SDFC, we are committed to the highest standards of ethics and integrity. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to provide innovative solutions and maintain the trust our customers place in us.

Key Features

  • Extensive network of branches and ATMs
  • Comprehensive range of banking products
  • Advanced online banking services
  • Commitment to financial inclusion
  • Social responsibility initiatives
